About Naggar Foreign Trade

Our Company offers effective, fast, reliable & customer oriented international logistics solutions to its customers with its experienced and dynamic staff and through its international partners Our vision means more to us than just a daring ambition. It describes our purpose as a company, and it sets an ambition level throughout the organisation. To fulfil this vision we have to be a leader within our industry in terms of innovation, the services we offer as well as the way we leverage the vast competence and experience embedded in the organisation. We offer integrated logistics solutions from factory to dealer through our operating companies and joint ventures. The logistics segment is organised in three business areas: technical services, inland distribution and supply chain management.
