Governance & Transparency

We believe transparency and governance are necessary in building a sustainable business model.



Rule number one is of course to comply with any local, regional and international law and regulation covering our business as well as ethical conduct.

Secondly, we will strive to be ahead of legislation as part of our ambition to shape the industry. We want to be at the forefront and contribute to making positive changes. All land-based employees were trained in anti-corruption behaviour Our governing elements give direction to how we must work. They are mandatory, at all levels in Naggar Foreign Trade company. They drive uniform performance and guide us in how we conduct our business, how we prioritise, and how we work internally.

For us, governance includes the common tools and methods we use to achieve the right results the right way. By working in a consistent manner throughout the network, we are able to navigate safely in a rapidly changing environment.
