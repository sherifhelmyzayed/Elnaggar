Naggar Foreign Trade History

Naggar Foreign Trade is a member of Naggar Group Companies established in 1989 and has been present in the shipping industry for over a century under different styles and names serving all aspects of shipping practice.

Naggar Foreign Trade was started by Tarek El Naggar in 2016 as Managing partner focusing on International Trading, logistics , warehousing and Consultancy.
