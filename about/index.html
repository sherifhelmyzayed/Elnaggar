Naggar Foreign Trade Long And Proud History.

Naggar Foreign Trade is a member of Naggar Group Companies established in 1989 and has been present in the shipping industry for over a century under different styles and names serving all aspects of shipping practice. The management today is the third generation of original founders, still providing the same high quality dedicated service we provided all over the 100 years with a modernised staff and facilities that take these services easily to ... the 21st century. Naggar Foreign Trade was started by Tarek El Naggar in 2016 as Managing partner focusing on International Trading, logistics , warehousing and Consultancy. Our professional experience in all segments of the business have created the proper opportunity to build the knowledge and staff necessary to handle and provide the best service to our clients. We are logistic experts in moving complex cargo, and we are now a part of the integrated supply chain for several auto and high and heavy manufacturers. We call it factory-to-dealer.
